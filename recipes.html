Tacos

Ingredients: 1 lb ground beef, 1 packet taco seasoning, taco shells, lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream.

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat. (Cook time: 7-10 minutes)

2. Add taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.

3. While the meat cooks, prepare taco toppings: shred lettuce, chop salsa, and grate cheese.

4. Warm taco shells in the oven for 5 minutes.

5. Assemble tacos by filling the shells with beef, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream.

Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Sushi Rolls

Ingredients: 2 cups sushi rice, 1 Â½ cups water, Â½ cup rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp salt, 10 nori sheets, fish (salmon or tuna), cucumber, avocado, soy sauce, wasabi.

Instructions:

1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions. While hot, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, and fold into the rice. Let rice cool.

2. Slice fish, cucumber, and avocado into thin strips.

3. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.

4. Lay fish, cucumber, and avocado across the middle. Roll tightly using the mat, sealing with a little water on the top edge.

5. Slice into 6-8 pieces. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Serving Size: 2 servings

Greek Salad

Ingredients: 3 cups chopped cucumber, 2 cups chopped tomatoes, 1 red onion, 1 cup Kalamata olives, 200g feta cheese, 3 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

1. Chop the cucumber, tomatoes, and onion into bite-sized pieces.

2. In a large bowl, combine the chopped vegetables, olives, and crumbled feta cheese.

3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.

4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently.

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients: 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade), 1 cup tomato sauce, 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, 1 tbsp olive oil, fresh basil, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 475Â°F (245Â°C).

2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired size.

3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

4. Tear mozzarella into pieces and scatter evenly over the sauce.

5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

6. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

Serving Size: 2 servings

Cheeseburger

Ingredients: 1 lb ground beef, 4 hamburger buns, 4 slices cheddar cheese, 1 onion (sliced), lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, ketchup, mustard, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium heat.

2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties, and season with salt and pepper.

3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, until cooked to your liking.

4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty in the last minute of cooking to melt.

5. Toast the buns on the grill or in a pan for a minute.

6. Assemble the burger by placing a patty on the bottom bun and adding your favorite toppings.

Cooking Time: 15-20 minutes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients: 1 cup broccoli florets, 1 cup sliced carrots, 1 bell pepper (sliced), 1 onion (sliced), 1/2 cup soy sauce, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tbsp sesame oil, garlic, ginger.

Instructions:

1. In a pan or wok, heat sesame oil over medium heat.

2. Add garlic and ginger, and sautÃ© for 30 seconds.

3. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, until tender-crisp.

4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and honey. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat.

Serving Size: 4 servings

Lasagna

Ingredients: 9 lasagna noodles, 1 lb ground beef, 2 cups ricotta cheese, 2 cups shredded mozzarella, 1 jar marinara sauce, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, herbs.

Instructions:

1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, then drain.

2. In a skillet, brown ground beef and garlic, then drain fat.

3. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan cheese, and herbs in a bowl.

4. In a baking dish, layer marinara sauce, lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat. Repeat layers.

5. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake at 375Â°F for 30 minutes, or until bubbly.

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Serving Size: 6 servings

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients: 400g spaghetti, 4 eggs, 1 cup Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup pancetta, 2 cloves garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.

2. In a skillet, sautÃ© pancetta and garlic in olive oil until crispy.

3. Whisk together eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.

4. Drain pasta, and toss with pancetta. Remove from heat.

5. Quickly toss pasta with the egg mixture to create a creamy sauce.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients: 4 chicken breasts, 1 tbsp olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, pepper.

Instructions:

1. Marinate chicken breasts with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and spices for at least 30 minutes.

2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

3. Grill chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.

4. Serve with your favorite sides.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1 cup mixed berries, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1 cup almond milk, honey to taste.

Instructions:

1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.

2. Blend until smooth and creamy.

3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Serving Size: 2 servings
